## Program Overview

Flexibility and mobility are often overlooked but essential components of fitness. This program focuses on improving your range of motion, reducing muscle tension, and preventing injuries through targeted stretching and mobility work.

## Why Flexibility Matters

- Reduces risk of injury
- Improves posture
- Enhances athletic performance
- Reduces muscle soreness
- Promotes relaxation and stress relief

## Daily Routine

### Morning Flow (10 minutes)

1. **Cat-Cow Stretch** - 10 repetitions
   - Start on hands and knees
   - Alternate between arching and rounding spine

2. **World's Greatest Stretch** - 5 per side
   - Lunge with rotation and reach

3. **Hip Circles** - 10 per direction
   - Standing or on all fours

4. **Shoulder Rolls** - 10 per direction
   - Slow, controlled movements

5. **Neck Stretches** - 30 seconds each side
   - Gentle ear to shoulder

### Evening Recovery (15 minutes)

1. **Pigeon Pose** - 1 minute per side
   - Deep hip stretch

2. **Seated Forward Fold** - 2 minutes
   - Hamstring and lower back

3. **Supine Twist** - 1 minute per side
   - Spinal rotation

4. **Figure Four Stretch** - 1 minute per side
   - Glute and hip opener

5. **Child's Pose** - 2 minutes
   - Full body relaxation

## Tips for Better Flexibility

- **Breathe deeply** - Exhale into the stretch
- **Never bounce** - Hold stretches steadily
- **Be consistent** - Daily practice yields best results
- **Warm up first** - Cold muscles don't stretch as well

## Who Is This For?

Perfect for anyone, from desk workers to athletes. This program complements any fitness routine and helps maintain a healthy, mobile body.

> "Flexibility is the key to stability."
