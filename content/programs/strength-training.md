## Program Overview

Our Strength Training program is designed to help you build lean muscle mass, increase your overall strength, and improve your body composition. Whether you're new to lifting or looking to take your training to the next level, this program provides a structured approach to achieving your goals.

## What You'll Learn

- Proper form and technique for compound movements
- Progressive overload principles
- Effective workout splits for muscle growth
- Recovery and nutrition basics

## Weekly Schedule

### Day 1: Upper Body Push
- Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-10 reps
- Overhead Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Tricep Dips: 3 sets x 12-15 reps

### Day 2: Lower Body
- Squats: 4 sets x 8-10 reps
- Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Leg Press: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
- Calf Raises: 4 sets x 15-20 reps

### Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery

### Day 4: Upper Body Pull
- Deadlifts: 4 sets x 6-8 reps
- Barbell Rows: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Pull-ups: 3 sets x max reps
- Face Pulls: 3 sets x 15-20 reps

### Day 5: Full Body
- Front Squats: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Lat Pulldown: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Core Circuit: 3 rounds

## Equipment Needed

- Barbell and weight plates
- Dumbbells
- Pull-up bar
- Bench

## Who Is This For?

This program is perfect for anyone who wants to build a stronger, more muscular physique. It's suitable for intermediate lifters who have mastered basic movements and are ready to progress.
