## Program Overview

The Full Body Fitness program is designed for beginners who want a comprehensive approach to fitness. Each workout targets all major muscle groups, ensuring balanced development and efficient use of your time.

## Program Benefits

- Balanced muscle development
- Efficient calorie burn
- Improved overall fitness
- Perfect for beginners
- Flexible scheduling

## Workout Structure

Each session follows this format:
- Warm-up: 5 minutes
- Main workout: 35 minutes
- Cool-down: 5 minutes

## Sample Full Body Workout

### Warm-Up
- Light jogging or jumping jacks: 2 minutes
- Dynamic stretches: 3 minutes

### Main Workout

**Circuit 1 - Upper Body (3 rounds)**
- Push-ups: 10-15 reps
- Dumbbell Rows: 12 reps per arm
- Shoulder Press: 12 reps
- Rest: 60 seconds

**Circuit 2 - Lower Body (3 rounds)**
- Bodyweight Squats: 15 reps
- Lunges: 10 per leg
- Glute Bridges: 15 reps
- Rest: 60 seconds

**Circuit 3 - Core (3 rounds)**
- Plank: 30-45 seconds
- Bicycle Crunches: 20 reps
- Superman: 12 reps
- Rest: 45 seconds

### Cool-Down
- Walking: 2 minutes
- Static stretches: 3 minutes

## Weekly Schedule

- **Monday**: Full Body Workout A
- **Tuesday**: Rest or light cardio
- **Wednesday**: Full Body Workout B
- **Thursday**: Rest or flexibility work
- **Friday**: Full Body Workout C
- **Weekend**: Active recovery

## Equipment Needed

- Dumbbells (light to medium weight)
- Exercise mat
- Water bottle

## Who Is This For?

Ideal for fitness beginners or those returning to exercise after a break. This program builds a solid foundation for more advanced training.

> "Every expert was once a beginner."
